How I ended up at Launchcode

Years on the job

I worked with kids with severe disabilities for over 18 years. I worked at a private day school, where kids that were not able to attend public shcool would go. They rode a bus to school everyday, it was not a residental facility. They could attend from the age of 3 to the day before their 21 birthday. This was not the place I planned on working, I did one of my clinicals there and never left. I worked with the same students durning my time here along with new ones as they started to attend. On average we lost 3 students a year. At some point I got tired of going to funerals for childern.

Opportunity Knocks

A few years back I had an opportuntiy for a job change. The job I worked for 18 years was very specific, even in the world of physical therapy so I never quite new if I would be able to transition into another facility. I thought this would be my time for change and I left for a new opportunity. Well to do some health issues (not mine) things did not work out they way they were planned. For this other role I went and got my health and life insurance license. Then as more changes were going on I was appointed to sell medicare plans. During which time I decided to take HR Block tax course. I wanted to better understand the impact taxes had on ones retirement. What would be the best for the clients worked with. I never intended to work there. But low and behold I will be starting my third tax season at HR Block.

So for the last 3 years basicly I have been taking test durning any free time I have. I have to renew my PTA license every two years with all the required continue education. The same year I had to complete the continued for insurance license in IL and MO along with multiple test to be recertified with each carry for medicare. And each year for taxes I need to complete 18 hour along with any course and test I want to complete to level up and increase my knowledge.

News Show

So I was watching the morning news last year and seen this people on there talking about Launchcode. I was amazed. I remember thinking, really this program will teach you code and there is not tuition or fees of any kind. Well of course between test and everythink I completely forgot. I kept thinking about the program but had forgot the name. Then a few month ago, I am watching the morning new and guess what came on, Launchcode. Of course, I almost forgot but I didn't! It was late on the last night you could apply and then here I am. It has been a chalange, nerve racking, and drives me crazy but I feel so pleased with myself when I complete an assignment. I have meet some really nice people, ones that I consider friends.
